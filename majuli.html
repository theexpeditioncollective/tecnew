MENTOR

PADMA SHRI HEMCHANDRA GOSWAMI

Mask Making

  • Padma Shri awardee (2023), recognised for preserving and advancing Majuli's traditional mask-making craft (mukha shilpa).
  • Based in Majuli and closely associated with the Samaguri Satra tradition for four decades, where masks are integral to Bhaona theatre and storytelling.
  • Trained under his father and guru, Rudra Kanta Deva Goswami, carrying forward a multi-generational lineage of the craft.
  • Known for creating highly expressive, performance-ready masks which has also won Damodara Deva National Award.
MENTOR

MAGDALENE JEYARATHNAM

Psychodrama

  • Dr Magdalene Jeyarathnam is a senior psychodramatist and educator with 30+ years of clinical, training, and institutional experience.
  • She founded East West Center for Counselling & Training (2007) and the Indian Institute of Psychodrama (2009), and served 7 years as a Board Director at the International Association of Group Psychotherapy and Group Processes.
  • Her work is known for integrating psychodrama with cultural ritual, story, and community-based frameworks rooted in the Indian context.
  • She pioneered Expressive Arts Therapy training in India, launching a Postgraduate Diploma with Women's Christian College in 2011 (still running annually).
  • Co-founded the Expressive Arts Therapy Association of India with Bakthi Veda Daul.
