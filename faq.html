A booking is confirmed only upon receipt of either the full payment or a non-refundable advance of ₹10,000. You may choose to pay the full amount at the time of booking. The tour will run only if a minimum of 10 participants register. If this number is not met, you will receive a full refund.

If the tour is confirmed and you choose to cancel:

  • The advance payment of ₹10,000 is non-refundable.
  • If you have paid the full amount, ₹10,000 will be deducted as the cancellation fee, and the balance will be refunded.
  • Refunds will be processed via bank transfer or as a credit note for a future TEC expedition. This will be decided on a case-by-case basis.
  • Refunds are processed within 10 working days.
