Be it the rush of rivers born in the Himalayas,

Or the echoes of prayers along Varanasi's ghats,

Be it the gleam in a Meenakari artisan's eye in Rajasthan,

Or the quiet rhythm of harvesters in Sikkim's fields.

Every soul holds a story untold,

Every hand, an art waiting to be embraced.

In this tapestry of voices, colors, and crafts,

We are woven together - living, breathing creators,

Boundless in our possibilities.

True understanding of the world comes through firsthand experiences - by immersing ourselves in new places, cultures, and perspectives. Travel, at its core, is one of the most enriching and fulfilling journeys life has to offer.

At the same time, reaching our fullest potential requires continuous learning - acquiring new skills, expanding our knowledge, and striving towards personal and professional growth.

But what if we could seamlessly blend the two? What if travel and learning came together to create transformative, well-rounded experiences that foster authentic growth?

That's exactly what we envision - a TRAVEL-AND-LEARN collective of like-minded explorers. Together, we cultivate meaningful experiences, develop valuable skills, revive our rich culture and build a thriving community filled with limitless opportunities.

Join us on this journey - where exploration meets growth, and where every step leads to a richer, more fulfilling life.

Founders

Roshni has been passionate about classical dance, painting, and singing from a young age, immersing herself in various art forms that have shaped her creative mindset. Eager to pursue her love for the arts, she traveled to Singapore and the UK to study art and design.

Her studies encompass fine arts, design, media, and art therapy, and she works on numerous art and design projects. Additionally, Roshni expanded her knowledge of art therapy through an online course at the University of Florida and trained under Alex and Allyson Grey in chakra therapy during her travels across the US.

Throughout her adult life, Roshni has dedicated herself to exploring the world and drawing inspiration from her experiences — believing the true value of learning lies in travel, understanding cultures, and learning from locals and their crafts.

Chaitanya has spent the last 7+ years chasing stories with his camera across bustling cities, quiet villages, and faraway corners of the world. After studying Architecture & Cinema, he returned to India and launched his own production house.

He's worked with India's top production houses and trained under legendary photographers. He was part of an international filmmaking crew, directing an award-winning documentary with nominations in Los Angeles, Mexico, Zambia, India, and California.

For Chaitanya, trekking, travelling, and storytelling are part of the same adventure. He aims to blend travel with learning, bringing together curious, creative people to explore, create, and leave every place a little better than they found it.
